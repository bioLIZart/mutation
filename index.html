BioART Poetry Slams

These slams are born from my recent travels across Asia and the ebb and flow of life itself. It's my first time diving into something like this, so I'd love to hear your thoughts, feedback, or just some kind words. Enjoyyyyy! :)))))

Yurt district Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia - 25/07/2024.
[note 22/11]: the slam is not published yet. Instead you'll hear some mongolian singing for now.
This slam is called *"Verweven Leven"* and it’s all about the deep bond between the Mongolian people and the land. Inspired by a ritual I witnessed, where a yurt is cleansed and ancestors are honored, it’s a tribute to the rhythm of nature and the connections we share with those who came before us. I hope it resonates with you in some way.

Random art gallery - Kazan, Russia - 24/08/2024.

On the cyanotype, you’ll find the phrase "Это прощание, да?", which translates from Russian to "This is goodbye, right?". It’s also the title of the slam, a reflection on the things I’m ready to say farewell to.
